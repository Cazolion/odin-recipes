Mac'N'Cheese Recipe

Description

Your favourite way to clog your arteries with lots of cream, lots of cheese, a couple more cheeses, and a bit more cheese.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Flour
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. In another pan, cook the cream and the flour
  3. Add cheddar until melted
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Combine
